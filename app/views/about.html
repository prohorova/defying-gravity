ABOUT

 My name is Allon Jacobs and I live in sunny California.  I attribute my flying addiction to my dad who let me ride along as he was doing unusual attitudes in a Cessna 210 over Los Angeles.  As soon as I saw that untied flight bag float above my head as the negative G's were hitting, I was hooked.  Fast forward a few decades and my 22 year old self poured every last cent of my summer construction job income into flight lessons at the University of California, Davis.  Sound familiar?

Recently, I realized when traveling domestically and internationally that I was having difficulty finding other pilots to speak with and share flying adventures with.  That's what sparked the idea to create a community where people could first find one another and then connect.

So if you haven't already, add your name to the pilots map, and let's see what happens.
